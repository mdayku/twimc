Narrative:
At approximately 14:30 hours, I responded to a traffic collision at the intersection of Main Street and Oak Avenue. Upon arrival, I observed a 2020 Honda Accord with significant damage to the driver's side and a 2019 Ford Transit delivery van with front-end damage.

Driver 1 (Sarah Johnson) stated she was traveling southbound on Main Street with a green traffic signal. As she entered the intersection, Driver 2's vehicle struck her vehicle on the driver's side.

Driver 2 (Michael Thompson) stated he was traveling eastbound on Oak Avenue when the collision occurred. Driver 2 admitted he "didn't see the red light" and failed to stop at the intersection.

Two witnesses at the scene corroborated Driver 1's account. Traffic camera footage was obtained showing Driver 2 entering the intersection against a red signal.

Driver 2 was cited for violation of CVC 21453(a) - Failure to Stop at Red Signal.
