Incident Description:
On January 15, 2024, at approximately 2:30 PM, Sarah Johnson was driving southbound on Main Street in Los Angeles, California. While proceeding through the intersection of Main Street and Oak Avenue with a green light, her vehicle was struck on the driver's side by a delivery truck operated by Michael Thompson, an employee of ABC Delivery Services, Inc. The defendant's vehicle ran a red light at high speed and collided with the plaintiff's vehicle, causing significant damage and injuries.
